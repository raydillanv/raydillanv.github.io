Office 5

  • Summary

    Protect your family. Protect your coworkers. Verify today. Office 5 is an analog indie horror experience where the player can never be certain who is human or who is unverified.

    Office 5 »

  • The Retroactive Fulfillment Corporation (The R.F.C.) exists in a world where Doppelgängers and unverified people plague society. 

    You are Riku Moriyama, a recent transfer from Office 3, who is starting his first day on the job at Office 5. Office 5 has recently secured its biggest client to date and has become inundated with transfers, new faces, and daily requirements for verification. 

  • Verification

  • The R.F.C. now recommends daily verification for all its employees.
  • "Protect your family. Protect your coworkers. Verify today."
  • "A verified employee is a safe employee."

    • Without verification there is no guarantee an employee will be granted access to any R.F.C. facilities. The admittance of unverified people endangers your coworkers, and most importantly, delays fulfillment of client requests. 

  • Office 5 Information

  • Office 5 is one of 32 branches of offices maintained by the R.F.C.
  • A new client recently chose Office 5 to be responsible for all of its requests.
  • In order to ensure consistent fulfillment, new employees and transfers must report to the Office 5 Verification Center. 

    • The top priority of Office 5 is to ensure the continuation of client request fulfillment, regardless of an employees circumstance. 

  • The Unverified Person Discrepancy Alarm (U.P.D.A.)

    In the unlikely event security detects the presence of an unverified person, the "U.P.D.A" will inform employees. Employees will be trained to escape to a safe place until help arrives. 

  • Key Features

  • Environment Interactions: Riku can interact with a variety of signs, items and NPCs to progress the story. 
  • Hiding: Get away from the enemy by utilizing your surroundings.
