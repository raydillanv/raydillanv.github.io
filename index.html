About Me

My name is Dillan Victory and I am currently in undergrad at Belmont University; I major in Asian studies and double minor in Computer Science and Japanese. In April of 2025, I published a research paper titled "The Economic and Environmental Impact of Shinkansen and High-Speed Rail Infrastructure: A Comparative Analysis of Economic Growth and Carbon Emissions Reduction". While this topic is still on my mind, in the past year and a half I have become very interested in game development, working with Unity and Unreal Engine, to release two games on Steam. In addition, the programming languages I have worked with include: Java, Python, C, C++, C#, Haskell, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and TypeScript. I also have a passion for learning languages, and have studied French (Intermediate) and Japanese (Advanced). In the future I hope to combine all three of my fields, Asian studies, computer science, and Japanese. Lastly, I am always open to new opportunities, so if you have any questions or would like to get in touch, please feel free to contact me.
